The Supreme Court Seems Likely To Overturn Roe v. Wade

May 3, 2022 According to a draft opinion obtained by POLITICO, there is a five-justice conservative majority ready to strike down Roe v. While, in theory, some justices could change their views before the ruling is formally issued, the leak signals a major shift in women's rights in the United States and in the norms and reputation of the Supreme Court.
